Luna

<
>

Luna came into Catitude as quite an aggressive, yet shy little kitty. Around her owners she's pretty confident and often displays anti-social behaviour such as clawing and biting regularly as well as making loud vocal complaints when she's picked up.

However when new people visit the household, she's runs and finds a hiding spot quicker than she runs to her food bowl at meal time. We're working on fixing her shyness and getting her to be more confident and open around strangers. This kind of therapy involves having her in controlled environments where we introduce her to new people every week. Luckily we have enough volunteers to keep each session fresh and new!

Luna's favourite treat is roast chicken, she could devour an entire chicken without hesitation. She's a bit of a diva and will refuse to eat any raw meat that isn't cooked, with the exception of fish. Her owners complain she's getting a little fat, but we can assure you she's in a pretty healthy weight for a cat of her age.
