Miu Miu is one of the more friendly cats we have coming through Catitude. He loves to wander around and put himself on display, adoring all the attention every one at the clinic is more than happy to give him.

Miu Miu came to us with a bit of an eating problemâ€¦ he loves to eat! You put anything down near him and hell gobble it up like a vacuum cleaner. Having an eating addiction isn't always an easy process to fix. He's a smart cat and knows when you're not feeding him enough food! The trick to this is giving him specially formulated cat food as well as introducing more often feeding, but less amount per session.

We haven't quite figured out what his favourite treat is. Anything put in his food bowl is consumed with the same gusto and enthusiasm.
